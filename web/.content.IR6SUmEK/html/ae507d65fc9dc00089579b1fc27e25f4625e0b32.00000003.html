AN EVOLUTION IN RECYCLING USED TIRES

Category Archives: Uncategorized

At Ecomills we believe in a greener earth for future generations,

our main raw material used in our production line is discarded and used tires.

We convert the used tires into beautiful entrance ways in the form of Tiles, pavers and matting.

We use natural coco fiber to make mats and matting.

Feel free to browse our website and take a look at our various natural coco and recycled tire products.
